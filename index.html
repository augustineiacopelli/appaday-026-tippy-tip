3 Quick Italian Tipping Reference
▼
🍽️ Restaurant (no servizio): 5–10% of bill, left as cash on the table.
🍽️ Restaurant (servizio included): No tip required — round up or leave €1–5 for exceptional service.
☕ Café at the counter: No tip expected. Standing orders don't warrant gratuity.
☕ Café with table service: €0.50–€1, or round up.
🏨 Hotel porter: €1–2 per bag.
🏨 Housekeeping: €1–2 per night, left daily.
🏨 Room service: Not required; €1–2 is a kind gesture.
🏨 Concierge: €5–10 routine; €15+ for difficult reservations.
🚕 Taxi: Round up. €2–5 for airport transfers.
🗺️ Tour guide (group): €2–5 per person.
🗺️ Tour guide (private): €30–100 depending on length and quality.
🚤 Gondola (Venice): Not expected; €5–10 if the experience was exceptional.
🚤 Water taxi (Venice): 5–10%, especially with luggage help.
🧥 Cloakroom: €0.75–€1 per item. Coins are fine here.
💅 Spa / Salon: Not required; up to 10% for exceptional service.
